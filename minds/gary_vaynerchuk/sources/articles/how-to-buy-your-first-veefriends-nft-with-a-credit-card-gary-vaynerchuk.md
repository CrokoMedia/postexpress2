## How to buy your first VeeFriends NFT on Rarible

1\. Go to [garyvee.com/vf](http://garyvee.com/vf), this will take you to a marketplace called Rarible that will showcase all VeeFriends collections: Including Series 1, Series 2, Iconics, and Book Games.   

![](https://garyvaynerchuk.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-22-at-1.12.40-PM-149x300.png)

2\. Choose the NFT that you like (Series 2 is our most accessible NFT collection). Click “Buy Now” on the NFT of your choice.   

![](https://garyvaynerchuk.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-22-at-1.14.38-PM-192x300.png)

3\. After clicking buy now, Click on “VeeFriends Wallet.” 

![](https://garyvaynerchuk.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-22-at-1.15.48-PM-216x300.png)

4\. Then, choose your sign-in method: **Google**, **Apple**, or **Email**.

If using email, you’ll receive a 6-digit verification code at the email address you entered. Enter it to confirm your login. Click “Approve” in the Privy pop-up to connect your VeeFriends Wallet to Rarible. Once approved, you’ll be logged in and ready to purchase.

_NOTE: If no approve window appears, your pop-up blocker might be blocking it. Turn it off and try again._  

![](https://garyvaynerchuk.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-22-at-1.17.57-PM-195x300.png)

5\. Click “Buy now” once more on the NFT of choice, and this time, complete checkout.  

You can pay with:  

You can check out with **Debit/Credit Card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay.**

If you cannot complete the transaction through Apple Pay or Google Pay, switch to manual card entry. If the charge continues to fail, reach out to your bank to approve the purchase.  
  
That’s it! Once the purchase is complete, your NFT is automatically transferred to your VeeFriends Wallet. 

## How to view your NFT

1\. Head over to [VeeFriends.com](http://veefriends.com/), click “get started” in the top right hand corner, and sign in **using the same email** you used in the prior steps to set up your Rarible VeeFriends wallet. 

2.  You’ll receive an authentication code by email; enter it when prompted. 

3\. Once you’re logged in, go to “collection”

![](https://garyvaynerchuk.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-11-22-at-1.24.17-PM-208x300.png)

4\. Here, you’ll see your newly purchased NFT!  
  
If you have any questions or don’t see your NFT in your account, please reach out to the team at [support@veefriends.com](mailto:support@veefriends.com). We’re happy to help!
