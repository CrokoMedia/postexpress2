# Want to work on Team GaryVee?

Hey everyone, this is Team GaryVee!

If you’re looking for a role, or have ever wanted to join the team behind GaryVee, keep reading!  
We’re currently looking for [passionate creatives](https://garyvee.com/tgvcreator), and [driven experts of social media](https://garyvee.com/applyteamgaryvee) to join our team as **[Creators](https://garyvee.com/tgvcreator) and** **[Platform Strategy Producers.](https://garyvee.com/applyteamgaryvee)  
Though, if you feel there’s a role that would better fit your skillset- [Fill out this form](https://garyvee.com/workwithus).**

[CLICK HERE TO APPLY](https://garyvee.com/workwithus) or keep reading to learn more.

Interested in the [**Platform Strategy Producer**](https://garyvee.com/applyteamgaryvee) position? [**CLICK HERE TO APPLY**](https://garyvee.com/workwithus) or keep reading to learn more:

**The Human:**

*   Has an ability to make relevant content across all platforms and has extraordinary skills in at least one if not all of these: producing, design, video editing, social media copywriting
*   Videography or design education OR equivalent relevant experience; all applicants will be asked to provide recent content examples from social media accounts
*   Shows a demonstrable passion, curiosity, and experience in ideating, strategizing, and making creative content on social media platforms – Instagram Reels, Instagram visuals, Tweets, TikTok videos, YouTube edits
*   Goes beyond basic tracking to analyze and draw hypotheses, including providing regular observations + recommendations on the next steps on how to break platforms
*   Proficient in one or many of the Adobe Creative Suite programs and eager to continue to learn and evolve in other efficient and effective ways to make social-first content: Adobe Creative Suite, mobile creator apps, and in-app editing (Instagram Story creation, TikTok editing, etc.)
*   Curious, responsible, accountable, and deeply passionate about their own personal interests and hobbies, and of course, kind, compassionate, and empathetic because you love the magic of collaboration

**The Gig:**

*   Producing & creating emotional, impactful, strategic organic content for Gary Vaynerchuk’s personal brand
*   Mastering the ability to conceptualize & strategize organic content  ideas and turn any branded content into a volume of social gold (clips, gifs, graphics, photos, stories, copy, etc.)
*   Managing & operating a platform(s)
*   Diving into analytics & data to create strategic organic content for the brand
*   Working with a wide range and quality of video and static imagery – from a collection of photography to short video clips – and turning them into content assets built for individual platforms and audiences
*   Collaborating with our creators to make and manipulate video, a plethora of short clips, photography, social copy, gifs, streaming stories, etc.
*   Honing your design, editing, and blurb-writing skills utilizing standard industry software and social platforms
*   Wrangling and rolling with your teammates to service rapid turnaround times, capture trending moments, and deliver and receive feedback, all while keeping a clear, enthusiastic, and proactive attitude towards content creation
*   Any experience using the following social listening & project management tools a plus but not mandatory:
    *   Asana
    *   Airtable
