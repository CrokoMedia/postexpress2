# Training Ellen's Palate - Gary Vaynerchuk

**Instant GaryVee Classic.**Back in ’07 when things were really blowing up for me, I had a chance to go on Ellen and run her through my awesome wine-tasting palate training. It was a ton of fun and I was super excited for everybody to see it… until the segment got cut from the show. Luckily, she showed the footage on the next episode. What’s even better is that I took her producer through it in advance she she totally crushed it! Big ups to Cara for eating the dirt!
